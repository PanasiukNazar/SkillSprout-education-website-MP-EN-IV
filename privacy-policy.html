Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal data and privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you access our educational resources and services. Please read this document carefully to understand how we manage your data.

1. Information Collection

We collect various types of information that enable us to provide and improve our educational services. This information may include:

  • Personal Information: Such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other data you provide when registering or contacting us.
  • Usage Information: Information about how you use our website, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and other analytical data.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your user experience and analyze how our site is used.

2. Use of Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To respond to your inquiries, facilitate registrations, and provide information about our educational services.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze and enhance the functionality of our website and services.
  • Communications: To send you information about updates, events, and special offers if you have consented to receive such communications.

3. Information Sharing

We will not sell, rent, or otherwise commercially exploit your personal information. However, we may share your information with the following third parties:

  • Service Providers: With third parties who assist us in providing our educational services, such as technical support and hosting.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, we may share your information in accordance with legal obligations or in response to a court order.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of part of our educational services, your information may be transferred to the new owner.

4. Information Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data. This includes the use of encryption, firewalls, and security protocols to safeguard your information against unauthorized access and misuse.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request Access: You can request access to your personal data that we hold at any time.
  • Correct Data: If you find that your data is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request its correction.
  • Withdraw Consent: If you have consented to the processing of your data, you can withdraw that consent at any time.
  • Request Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal data if it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.

6. Cookies

We use cookies on our site to track your activity and improve your user experience. You can modify your cookie settings in your browser, but please note that this may affect the functionality of our site.

7. Links to Third Parties

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. These sites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content. We recommend that you read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

8. Changes to the Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with the current date. We recommend checking this page regularly to stay informed of any changes.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy, please do not hesitate to contact us. Your privacy is important to us, and we are happy to provide you with any necessary information and support.

10. Final Provisions

This Privacy Policy constitutes the entire agreement between you and us regarding the protection of your personal data. Any provision found to be invalid will not affect the validity of the remaining provisions of this policy.
